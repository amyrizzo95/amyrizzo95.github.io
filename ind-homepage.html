BFA Online Exhibition 2022

This is a collaborative digital arts exhibition that features our final dissertations and different artistic work.

Every artist has independently developed and selected a range of pieces that come together in a complex and precisely planned art creation, that is backed by comprehensive study in the appropriate topic. No two projects are the identical, since each focuses on a theme that the artist is particularly enthusiastic in exploring, portrayed via their favorite medium/media of choice.
